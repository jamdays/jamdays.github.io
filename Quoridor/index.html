Quoridor

Get your litte guy to the other side first and you win

Rules (unofficial)

  1. You can move left, right, forwards, or backwards, one square at a time
    1. if you are next to your opponent you can jump over them
    2. if there is a wall in that direction you can jump to their sides
  2. You can place a wall in between any tiles (that aren't taken already)
    1. No jumping over walls
    2. You always have to leave a path for the other player to reach the other side
  3. You win if you get to the other side before your opponent
