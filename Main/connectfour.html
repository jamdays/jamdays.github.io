Connect Four

Connect Four, a fun version of tic-tac-toe, because there is gravity (too real)
oh yeah, and you aren't just connecting 3, like in tic tac toe! (You are connecting 6 tokens!)

Rules (not official, again, from memory)

  1. Choose a column to drop your token in
  2. Your token then falls to the lowest open spot (gravity)
  3. Then it is the next person's turn!
  4. First person to connect 3 tokens wins!
