Blokus

You're either blokus or against us

Rules (unofficial)

  1. You start from the corner, and place one block, from your corner
  2. Any block you place afterwards has to be attached at a corner to another one of your blocks
  3. But it cannot be adjacent to any of your other blocks
  4. Whoever has the least blocks left wins (as in least squares)
  5. To rotate blocks, click them, to mirror blocks, shift click
